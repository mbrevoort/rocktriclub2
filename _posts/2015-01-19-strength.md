* Warm up 10 minutes- your choice
* As Many Rounds As Possible (AMRAP in 25 minutes):
* 1x around track slow, 1x medium, 1x fast
* 10 pushups
* 15 overhead presses
* 20 jump squats
* 10 burpees
* Cool down 5 minutes and stretch
