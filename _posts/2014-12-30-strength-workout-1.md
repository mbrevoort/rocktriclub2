* 10 minute warm up
* ARAP (as many rounds as possible):
    - 50 jump rope or 1/4 mile run
    - 10 overhead presses
    - 20 squats
    - 30 bicycle crunches
    - plank for 60 seconds
    - 10 burpees
* 10 minutes cool down
